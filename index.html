About the club

Smart Club since 2018 was originally established as a private goal-setting club in Toronto by amazing and very intelligent girl Elena Kosenko. However, in 2021 she moved to California and passed the club to Mila Bogorodovska. After that the club started to expand its membership and we organized more than 100 events in Toronto. Our most famous events include White Parties on a 100ft Sailing Boat, vine tours in castles, and fun networking events. If you want to participate in our events you can simply DM us on Instagram fill out the form here.
Since 2021, we have welcomed over 250 women to our events. During this time, we have also collaborated with numerous companies and individuals who wanted to promote their services. If you're looking to expand the reach of your business and increase its visibility, we would be delighted to explore potential collaboration opportunities with you. To begin the conversation, simply reach out to us via email , and we can schedule a time to discuss how we can work together to achieve our respective goals. We look forward to the possibility of building a mutually beneficial partnership with you.

Vision

Our vision is to create a vibrant community of women who come together to enjoy life, build meaningful connections, and create unforgettable memories. We believe that through fun and engaging events, we can foster a sense of belonging, promote positive relationships, and inspire women to lead fulfilling lives. We strive to be a dynamic organization that offers a diverse range of activities and experiences that cater to the interests and passions of our members. Our ultimate goal is to create a community that brings joy, happiness, and laughter into the lives of all women who join us.

Mission

Our mission is to empower and support women by creating a community where members can connect, learn, and grow together. We aim to provide a safe and inclusive space where women can share their experiences, exchange knowledge and skills, and inspire each other to achieve their personal and professional goals.

Meet the founder

Mila Bogorodovskaya
She is a former model, business developer, and online educator with 10.5K students around the globe. She also holds an MSc in Mathematics. As an international model, Mila has traveled across the globe and lived and worked in 17 countries, including cities like Milan, New York, Paris, Miami, Istanbul, and Mexico City. She has been featured on over 20 magazine covers and inside notable publications such as Vogue Italy, Women's Health, and L'Officiel, to name a few. Mila is passionate about solving problems, piecing together customers' problems with relevant solutions through discovery, analysis, and intuition. That's why she smoothly transitioned into a business development career for companies in FinTech five years ago. Mila believes that everything is possible and has always been interested in self-development. She became a Reiki master and finished certain schools where she learned how to work with energy to make sure goals will come true. This led to her coaching business owners from 2016-2018. Now, some of those techniques are used during SCC meetings. Mila is married and raising a son.

Our club members

Our club members are absolutely amazing, filled with intelligence, grace, and charm. These incredible women embody strength and balance in all aspects of their lives, from managing careers to nurturing their loved ones and pursuing their passions
When our club members gather for our parties, they create an atmosphere of warmth, friendship, and connection. It's a space where they can come together to share stories, exchange ideas, and build meaningful relationships. Each gathering is an opportunity for them to learn something new, whether it's about healthy living, fitness, self-care, psychology or spirituality, real estate, cosmetology, skincare, or fashion.
Beyond their dedication to personal growth and self-care, our club members also love to have fun and explore the world. They're passionate about traveling to new places, trying out the latest restaurants, and making unforgettable memories. It's this adventurous spirit and zest for life that makes our community so special.
Overall, our club members are truly remarkable individuals, and we are grateful to have them as a part of our community.

Our events

Monthly meeting

At our monthly meetings, we focus on goal-setting and analyzing previous achievements. These events take place in restaurants or event facilities in Toronto and often feature an exciting activity or a guest speaker. We make sure each meeting is a learning opportunity, a chance to connect and have fun. We talk about investment, art, healthy habits, new business opportunities, and much more.
In addition to our monthly meetings, we also organize retreats and mini-trips to beautiful destinations like Niagara on the Lake or Lavender Farms. We love to celebrate special occasions too, whether it's Women's Day or the start of summer. Also, we do different creative workshops like painting or making Christmas Arrangements. Please check out Instagram for the next available events.

White party on the sailing boat

For instance, every summer, we host a White Party on a 100 ft boat that sails from downtown Toronto to the Islands. It's a great opportunity to combine sailing with a fun party atmosphere. Last year, we had a spectacular fashion show on the boat, featuring the clothing brand Moon Rivers Fashion. Our guests were able to become models and showcase beautiful dresses. We also had feminine dancing sessions, mini photoshoots, and delicious snacks, making it four hours of pure enjoyment.

Woman's day celebration
Main sponsor: Korean cosmetic brand Atomy

We also hosted a wonderful Women's Day celebration, sponsored by the Korean cosmetic brand Atomy. Each girl received a special gift from them and was able to enjoy a Dolce and Gabbana set up from one of our sponsors. We had dancing, charcuterie snacks, photo-taking, and of course, more dancing.

Organizing party for the flamingo collective store

Another event we organized was a costume Halloween Party at Flamingo Store Collective, one of our sponsors' facilities in Distillery District. We had a competition for the best costume, and the winner received a gift from Mac Cosmetics. We also had fun contents for everyone to enjoy.
