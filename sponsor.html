Collaborations

White party on the sailing boat

Every summer, we hold a White Party on a 100 ft boat that takes us from downtown Toronto to the Islands. We combine sailing with a fun party atmosphere. Last year, we had a spectacular fashion show on the boat featuring the clothing brand Moon Rivers Fashion. Our guests were able to become models and showcase beautiful dresses. As a result, 10 dresses were sold on the spot, and thanks to the increased amount of followers on Instagram, their sales increased by 20%.

Organizing party for the flamingo collective store

There is a new gift store located in the Distillery District that is looking to increase its exposure. To help with this, we recently organized a fun Halloween party at the store. Our guests enjoyed the wide variety of gifts that were on display and had a great time. We shared a lot of Instagram stories about the store on our social media platforms and purchased some items ourselves.

Woman's day celebration
Main Sponsor: Korean cosmetic brand Atomy

Woman's day celebration
Main sponsor: Korean cosmetic brand Atomy

We created a beautiful atmosphere in one of Atomy's offices for a promotional event. During the party, we showcased Atomy's SPA treatment on a model, and all the attendees were able to try the products and experience the benefits immediately. Atomy also provided useful gifts for everyone at the event. As a result of the event, interested people subscribed to Atomy's website and made purchases on the same day. Overall, the event was a success in terms of promoting Atomy's products and generating sales. By creating an engaging and positive atmosphere and offering demonstrations and giveaways, we were able to attract potential customers and encourage them to try out Atomy's offerings.
